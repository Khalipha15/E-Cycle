Why Recycle E-Waste?

E-waste is one of the fastest-growing waste streams globally. By recycling your old electronics, you help reduce pollution, conserve natural resources, and minimize the environmental impact of discarded devices.

Protect the Environment

Electronics contain hazardous materials like mercury and lead that can pollute our land and water.

Save Natural Resources

Recycling recovers valuable materials like gold, copper, and plastic, reducing the need for mining new resources.

Reduce Carbon Footprint

Recycling electronics reduces greenhouse gas emissions and helps conserve energy used in manufacturing new products.

Our Impact

We aim to recycle over 10,000 devices this year, preventing over 5 tons of harmful waste from ending up in landfills.
