Frequently Asked Questions

What types of electronics can I recycle?

We accept most types of electronics, including phones, laptops, computers, tablets, TVs, and more. For a full list, please visit our Services page.

Is there a fee for pickup services?

No, we offer free e-waste pickup services for most areas. Please check availability in your location.

How does the recycling process work?

Your devices are carefully sorted, dismantled, and recycled according to eco-friendly practices. Any hazardous materials are safely disposed of.
